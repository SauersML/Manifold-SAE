## Run 0 — Synthetic recovery gate

- `manifold_falsifier.py --selftest` passed on the A100: Procrustes scoring and sigma-min diagnostics are valid.
- Pure-torch `py_recover.py` is running on the A100 as a solver-independent coherence sweep with oracle gates.
- A reduced ultrafast torch proxy produced immediate recovery/cliff signal:
  - orthogonal/easy (`coh=0.00`, `sigma_min=0.940`): coordinate R² `0.984`, reconstruction R² `0.970`.
  - near-colinear/hard (`coh=0.95`, `sigma_min=0.760`): coordinate R² falls to `0.716` while reconstruction R² remains `0.972`, exposing the reproducible-but-wrong failure mode.
  - decoder-incoherence weight needs tuning in this proxy: at `coh=0.95`, `lambda=0.10` improves coordinate R² from `0.716` to `0.754`, while `lambda=1.0` is too aggressive and hurts recovery.
